I-Land Survival Show

Overview CJ ENM and BIGHIT, the founders of BELIFT LAB, join together to discover and train K-pop artists. I-LAND is a survival reality show where 23 trainees compete to debut in a new K-pop boy group. It is a space full of the newest and best technology where the I-Landers from all over the world can practice and perform. With limited time, the I-Landers must pass the tests presented to them by the system.
Genre Music, Reality
Release Date Jun. 25. 2020 - Sep. 17. 2020
Director Jeong Min-seok, Kim Sin-young, Lee Seon-yeong

(July 3, 2020)		 Theme Song (part 1) "Into the I-LAND" parts assigned; producers evaluate I-Landers individually; group average used to decide moves. I-Land performers selected; Lee Hee-seung initially leader (later gives to Ni-Ki). I-Land average low. Chooses and evaluates parts / performers
Episode 3
(July 10, 2020)		 Theme Song (part 2) Final theme song performances and producer evaluation. I-Land group average = 59 → must move out six contestants. Several contestants are chosen to be moved out from I-Land (based on scores & votes). Those kept: (some saved; group reshuffle) Grounders remain under review
Episode 4
(July 17, 2020)		 Teamwork I-Land & Ground perform BTS "Fire" remix (six days prep). Highest individual I-Lander gets a dropout exemption card. I-Land teamwork score high/average; top scorer uses exemption to save a member; multiple reshuffles occur. K (used exemption), other high scorers saved Ground leaders Jay & Ni-Ki; some Ground→I-Land swaps
Episode 5
(July 24, 2020)		 Representative Unit Vocal & dance reps chosen to battle Ground. If Ground wins, six I-Land members would be dropped. I-Land wins the representative unit battle → no additional demotions at this stage. Vocal/Dance reps performed well; I-Land total > Ground total Ground loses this round
Episode 6
(July 31, 2020)		 Final 12 (Part 1 finale) "I&credible" final performance (theme: Awakening). Producers + I-Land votes decide final 12 who advance to Part 2; global voting to fill remaining spots. Six I-Land members are saved automatically; producers and global votes finalize the initial Final 12 lineup. Saved: K, Lee Hee-seung, Park Sung-hoon, Yang Jung-won, Jake, Jay (examples) Demoted/selected Grounders join via global vote
Part 2 Episode 7
(Aug 14, 2020)		 BTS Test (Unit Performance) Final 12 split into 3 units to perform BTS songs. Unit with highest team score is exempt from elimination. Freestyle mini-mission ranking determines unit leaders. Units: "Fake Love" (1st) — Taki, Park Sung-hoon, Lee Hee-seung, Kim Sun-oo; top individual ranks earn privileges. Elimination based on individual scores; Lee Geon-u eliminated (example)
Episode 8
(Aug 21, 2020)		 BTS Test / Results Detailed individual & unit scoring finalised; low individual in non-winning unit eliminated, but exemptions can save members. K, Yang Jung-won, Lee Hee-seung among top scorers in different rounds Taki faced elimination but saved as a member of the winning unit (example)
Episode 9
(Sept 4, 2020)		 Chemistry Test Contestants perform "Flicker" and "Dive Into You"; global votes decide eliminations. First-place contestant chosen by producers gets vote multiplier. Producer pick: Jake (helps unit performance); Lee Hee-seung placed first and doubled votes window. Eliminations based on global voting results (lowest-ranked eliminated)
Episode 10
(Sept 11, 2020)		 Concept Test Concept & mini-mission (Seventeen covers) followed by main concept performances ("Chamber 5" & "Flame On"). Units are judged and lowest individual eliminated. Kim Sunoo ranks first in this stage; K also performs strongly. Taki and/or Hanbin face elimination in this phase (based on ranking)
Episode 11
(Sept 11, 2020)		 Concept Test / Final Prep Final preparations and last evaluations by producers; contestants finalize PR videos and stage details for live final. Top-ranked contestants secure longer PR times and positioning for live final. Contestants in danger repositioned or attempt last improvements
Episode 12
(Sept 18, 2020)		 Final Test (Live) Live broadcast final performance ("Calling (Run Into You)"); global votes + producer pick determine debut lineup of 7 members. Final debuted members (ENHYPEN): Yang Jung-won, Jay, Jake, Ni-Ki, Lee Hee-seung, Park Sung-hoon — plus Kim Sun-oo (producer's pick). Previously eliminated trainees return for joint performance; final eliminations announced live.
